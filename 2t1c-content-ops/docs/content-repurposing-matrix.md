## The Repurposing Philosophy

Every idea is raw material. Every clip is reusable fuel. The goal is **maximum extraction** — not "one idea, one post" but "one idea, every viable angle."

Three layers:
- **Layer 1: TEXT FORMAT** — Structural shape (Stat Bomb, Thread, Bark QRT, etc.)
- **Layer 2: MEDIA ENHANCEMENT** — Visual/video asset (Clip, GIF, screenshot, multi-clip)
- **Layer 3: EDITORIAL ANGLE** — Spin (Irony, scale, absurdity, explainer, debate)

Any text format can combine with any media type. Any idea can take multiple editorial angles.

## The 3-Question Framework

For every idea that passes filtering:

**Q1: What text formats can this idea support?**
Run through: Stat Bomb? Bark QRT? Tuki QRT? Thread? Contrarian Take? Commentary? Explainer? One-liner? GIF React? Roundup? Video Clip Post?

**Q2: What media assets exist or can be created?**
Video to clip? Related YouTube clips? Screenshot? GIF? Infographic?

**Q3: How many distinct angles can we take?**
Straight news, hot take, contrarian, explainer, historical context, debate, human interest, pattern recognition.

**Total possible posts = Viable Formats × Available Media × Distinct Angles**

---

## Clip Reuse Rules

- Every clip should appear in minimum 2 posts
- From one 15-minute video, aim to extract 3-5 clips
- Don't reuse the exact same clip more than 3 times
- Cross-source clips (from related videos on same topic) add depth

### Clip Selection Criteria
1. **The Money Quote** — Most striking statement (15-30s)
2. **The Demo Moment** — Visual proof (20-45s)
3. **The Reaction** — Genuine surprise/shock (10-20s)
4. **The Explainer Bite** — Clearest explanation (30-60s)
5. **The Contradiction** — Opposite of current reality (15-30s)
