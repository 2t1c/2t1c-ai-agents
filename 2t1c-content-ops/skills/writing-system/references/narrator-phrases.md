# Narrator Phrases Reference

Use 2-3 per thread maximum. Position determines the job, not the phrase itself. Any phrase can open a hook, appear mid-thread, or close.

---

## Job 1 — Pacing Reset
After dense information sections. Gives the reader permission to slow down.

- Stay with me on this one...
- Bear with me. This gets wild.
- Stick with me through the first part...
- Read this carefully...
- Read every line of this carefully...
- Don't skip ahead on this one...
- Give this one a chance. The ending will surprise you.
- This one builds slowly but the payoff is worth it.
- Set aside 3 minutes for this one...
- Give me 60 seconds and I'll change how you see this forever...
- This one is worth your full attention...
- Clear your head before reading this...

---

## Job 2 — Anticipation Builder
Before a major reveal. Creates the pause that makes the next line hit harder.

- This is where the story completely changes...
- And then it gets even better...
- This is the detail that changes everything...
- And here's where it gets interesting...
- This is the part nobody tells you...
- This is the moment everything shifted...
- This is where it stops being a history lesson...
- This is the part that got me...
- I promise this is going somewhere unexpected...
- Trust me, you want to see where this goes...

---

## Job 3 — Credibility Through Vulnerability
Admit uncertainty or genuine surprise. Builds trust by refusing to be the all-knowing narrator.

- I genuinely don't know how this ends...
- This kept me up at night...
- Honestly this humbled me more than I expected...
- I've spent weeks trying to make sense of this...
- I went down a rabbit hole on this and never came back...
- I had no idea this existed until last week...
- I've never seen anything quite like this...
- This completely changed how I think about...
- This is the one topic I keep coming back to...
- I didn't want to stop reading once I started...

---

## Job 4 — Permission to Feel
Immediately after a surprising fact. Validates the reader's disbelief.

- I swear this has to be...
- This broke my brain when I first found it...
- I genuinely can't believe this is real...
- This might be the most insane thing I've ever researched...
- I've read this a dozen times and it still doesn't feel real...
- I almost didn't believe this when I first read it...
- This shouldn't be possible. But it happened.
- This is the kind of story that makes you question everything...
- No one talks about this and I genuinely don't understand why...

---

## Job 5 — Stakes Elevation
Before the close or final insight. Signals that this matters beyond the story.

- This matters more than most people realize...
- The world looks different after you understand this...
- File this one away. It's going to matter.
- The implications of this are still unfolding...
- Not many people have connected these dots yet...
- Most people will scroll past this...
- This is quietly one of the most important stories of our time...
- This is the kind of thing that changes slowly then all at once...
- Most people won't see this coming...
- The surface story is interesting. What's underneath it is everything...
- This is the context missing from every conversation about this topic...
- The story everyone knows is only half of it...
